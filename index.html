Instructions for Diagnosing Myocarditis

  1. Give 5 points for presence of inflammation on either MRI, PET or Biopsy.
  2. Give 3 points for raised CRP (CRP>3).
  3. Give 1 point for presence of Chest Pain.

The Results will indicate Definite or Probable Myocarditis or if it is Unlikely.
